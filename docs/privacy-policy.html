Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 27, 2026 (Version 2.0 - AI Training Update)

Important Update: We now collect and use your queries to train and improve our AI models. By using this app, you consent to your queries being used for AI training purposes. We do not collect personal information and you can opt-out of data collection.

1. Introduction

Text to SQL AI ("we," "our," or "the app") is an AI-powered application that converts natural language questions into SQL queries. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. AI Processing and Training Disclosure

This app uses artificial intelligence to process your queries AND collects your queries to train and improve AI models. Before using the app, you must consent to both AI processing and data collection as required by Apple's 2026 App Store guidelines.

What this means:

  • Your text queries are sent to third-party AI services (Hugging Face) for processing
  • Your queries and the generated SQL are stored in our database for AI training
  • We collect your feedback (thumbs up/down, corrections) to improve model accuracy
  • Your data is used to fine-tune AI models to generate better SQL queries
  • No personal identification data (name, email, etc.) is required or collected
  • You can opt-out of data collection (see Section 8)

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide

  • Query Text: The natural language questions you type into the app (STORED FOR AI TRAINING)
  • Generated SQL: The SQL queries generated by the AI (STORED FOR AI TRAINING)
  • User Feedback: Your ratings (thumbs up/down) and corrections (STORED FOR AI TRAINING)
  • Comments: Optional feedback comments you provide (STORED FOR AI TRAINING)
  • Consent Records: Your acceptance of AI processing and data collection (stored locally on your device)

3.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Session ID: Anonymous identifier for your app session (not linked to personal identity)
  • Timestamps: When queries were submitted and feedback was provided
  • Query Success Metrics: Whether the SQL executed successfully or failed
  • Error Messages: Technical error details when queries fail
  • Model Version: Which AI model version was used for each query
  • Usage Data: Basic app functionality logs (errors, crashes)
  • Device Information: iOS version, device type (for app compatibility)

3.3 Information We DO NOT Collect

  • Name, email, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access
  • Contacts or calendar data
  • Financial or payment information

4. How We Use Your Information

  • AI Query Processing: To convert your questions into SQL queries in real-time
  • Model Training: To train and improve AI models for better SQL generation accuracy
  • Quality Improvement: To analyze feedback and identify common query patterns
  • Performance Metrics: To measure model accuracy and track improvements over time
  • App Functionality: To display results and improve user experience
  • Technical Support: To diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Research and Development: To develop better text-to-SQL algorithms

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Hugging Face

We use Hugging Face's AI inference API to process your queries. When you submit a question:

  • Your query text is sent to Hugging Face servers for processing
  • Hugging Face processes the text using AI models
  • The generated SQL query is returned to the app
  • Hugging Face may log API requests per their privacy policy
  • Note: We separately store your queries in our database for training our own models

Hugging Face Privacy Policy

6. Data Storage and Retention

  • Local Storage: Your AI consent preference is stored locally on your device using localStorage
  • Cloud Storage: We store your queries, generated SQL, and feedback in our training database
  • Database Location: Training data is stored on our Hugging Face Space servers
  • Retention Period: Training data is retained indefinitely to improve AI models over time
  • Anonymization: All stored data is anonymous (no personal identifiers linked)
  • Data Deletion Rights: You can request deletion of your data (see Section 8.2)

6.1 What Data is Stored

  • Natural language queries
  • Generated SQL queries
  • User feedback (thumbs up/down, corrections)
  • Query execution success/failure status
  • Anonymous session identifiers
  • Timestamps

6.2 What Data is NOT Stored

  • Your name, email, or contact information
  • IP addresses (beyond session duration)
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Any personal identification information

7. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • All connections use HTTPS encryption
  • No persistent storage of query content
  • Minimal data collection principle

8. Your Rights and Choices

8.1 AI Processing and Training Consent

  • You must consent to AI processing and data collection to use the app
  • You can decline consent (the app will not function without it)
  • You can withdraw consent at any time (see opt-out instructions below)
  • You can reset your consent by reinstalling the app

8.2 Opt-Out of Data Collection

IMPORTANT: If you want to opt-out of having your queries used for AI training:

  • Contact us at privacy@texttosql.app with your Session ID
  • Your Session ID is displayed at the bottom of the app
  • We will exclude your queries from future training datasets
  • Note: Opting out means the app still works, but your data won't improve the AI

8.3 Data Deletion Requests

  • Local Data: Delete the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Training Data: To delete your queries from our training database:
    • Email privacy@texttosql.app with your Session ID
    • We will delete all queries associated with that session within 30 days
    • Deleted data will not be used in future model training
    • Note: Data already used in trained models cannot be "un-trained"
  • Right to Erasure (GDPR): EU residents have the right to request complete data deletion

8.4 Data Access Requests

  • You can request a copy of all data we've collected from your session
  • Email privacy@texttosql.app with your Session ID
  • We will provide a JSON export of your query history within 30 days

9. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not require age verification.

10. International Users

This app is available worldwide. By using the app, you consent to the transfer of your query data to Hugging Face servers, which may be located outside your country of residence.
