Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 27, 2026

Your Privacy Matters: Text to SQL AI is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data and do not sell your information to third parties.

1. Introduction

Text to SQL AI ("we," "our," or "the app") is an AI-powered application that converts natural language questions into SQL queries. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. AI Processing Disclosure

This app uses artificial intelligence to process your queries. Before using the app, you must consent to AI processing as required by Apple's 2026 App Store guidelines.

What this means:

  • Your text queries are sent to third-party AI services (Hugging Face)
  • The AI processes your questions to generate SQL queries
  • No personal identification data is required or collected

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide

  • Query Text: The natural language questions you type into the app
  • Consent Records: Your acceptance of AI processing (stored locally on your device)

3.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Basic app functionality logs (errors, crashes)
  • Device Information: iOS version, device type (for app compatibility)

3.3 Information We DO NOT Collect

  • Name, email, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access
  • Contacts or calendar data
  • Financial or payment information

4. How We Use Your Information

  • AI Query Processing: To convert your questions into SQL queries
  • App Functionality: To display results and improve user experience
  • Technical Support: To diagnose and fix technical issues

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Hugging Face

We use Hugging Face's AI inference API to process your queries. When you submit a question:

  • Your query text is sent to Hugging Face servers
  • Hugging Face processes the text using AI models
  • The generated SQL query is returned to the app
  • Your queries are not used to train AI models

Hugging Face Privacy Policy

6. Data Storage and Retention

  • Local Storage: Your AI consent preference is stored locally on your device using localStorage
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your queries on our servers
  • Retention Period: Query data is not retained after processing

7. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • All connections use HTTPS encryption
  • No persistent storage of query content
  • Minimal data collection principle

8. Your Rights and Choices

8.1 AI Processing Consent

  • You can decline AI processing (the app will not function without it)
  • You can reset your consent by reinstalling the app

8.2 Data Deletion

  • Delete the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Your queries are not stored, so there's no historical data to delete

9. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not require age verification.

10. International Users

This app is available worldwide. By using the app, you consent to the transfer of your query data to Hugging Face servers, which may be located outside your country of residence.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Legal Compliance

  • Apple App Store Guidelines (2026)
