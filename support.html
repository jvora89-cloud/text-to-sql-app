Frequently Asked Questions

What is Text to SQL AI?
Text to SQL AI is an app that converts your natural language questions into SQL database queries using artificial intelligence. It's designed to make database queries accessible to everyone, even without SQL knowledge.
Is my data private?
Yes! We don't store your queries permanently. Your questions are processed by AI to generate SQL, then discarded. Your consent preference is stored locally on your device. Read our Privacy Policy for details.
Why does the app need AI consent?
Apple's 2026 App Store guidelines require apps that use AI to obtain explicit user consent before processing data. This ensures transparency about how your data is used.
What database does the app use?
The app includes a demo database with 70 real companies from Fortune 500 and AI industries. It includes 7 tables: Companies, Departments, Employees, Customers, Products, Projects, and Transactions.
Can I use my own database?
Currently, the app uses a fixed demo database. Custom database support may be added in future versions based on user feedback.
The app won't load / shows an error
Try these troubleshooting steps:
  1. Check your internet connection - the app requires internet to function
  2. Force quit and restart the app
  3. Make sure you've accepted the AI consent modal
  4. Wait a few minutes - the backend service may be waking up
  5. If the issue persists, contact support below
How do I reset my AI consent?
Delete and reinstall the app. Your consent preference is stored locally, so reinstalling will reset it and show the consent modal again.
Example questions I can ask
  • "Show me all technology companies"
  • "Which company has the most employees?"
  • "List all employees in the Engineering department"
  • "What are the total transactions by company?"
  • "Show customers from California"
  • "Which products cost more than $500?"
Which AI service does the app use?
The app uses Hugging Face's AI inference API with models like Mistral-7B. Your queries are processed through Hugging Face's servers to generate SQL queries.
Does the app work offline?
No, an internet connection is required because the app loads its interface from Hugging Face Spaces and uses AI processing that requires network access.
